Accessibility statement: Orange Digital Accessibility Guidelines Web Site

Context

Orange SA continues to expand inclusive and accessible services so more people can benefit from digital technology and feel confident using it.

Orange is commited to make its internet, intranet, extranet websites and its mobile applications accessible in accordance with article 47 of Law No. 2005-102 of February 11, 2005.

In order to do so, Orange implements the following strategy and actions:

Digital Accessibility Multi-year Plan 2024 - 2026.

Actions performed over the years and action plan for the current year.

This accessibility statement applies to Orange Digital Accessibility Guidelines Web Site.

Conformity level:

"Orange Digital Accessibility Guidelines Web Site" is partially compliant to the criteria with WCAG version 2.2, level AA.

ORANGE SA refers to the WCAG international recommendations version 2.2, level AA. Here is the correspondence link between the RGAA and the WCAG (french).

The analyze was carried out via la Va11ydette.

Feedback and contact information

If you are unable to access content or a service, you can contact the site manager to be directed to an accessible alternative or obtain the content in another form.

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this site. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility problems by sending an email to accessibility.group@orange.com.

Legal remedies

You have reported to the website manager an accessibility problem that prevents you from accessing content or one of the portal's services and you have not received a satisfactory response, in this case:

  • Write a message to the Defender of Rights.
  • Or contact the Defender of Rights delegate in your department.
  • Or send a letter by post (free of charge, do not put a stamp)
