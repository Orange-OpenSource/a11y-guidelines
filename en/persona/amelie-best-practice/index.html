Good digital accessibility practices for Amélie

Good Reflexes Common Mistakes
Form
Provide an explicit title for each form field
Use non-explicit error messages and do not indicate required fields
Screen Reader
Provide alt text for informative images
Do not report language changes
Captcha
Provide an alternative to Captcha
Use Captchas to prevent spam
Office document
Use the “Check accessibility” function
Do not structure the document with “title” styles
Links
Provide meaningful wording for hyperlinks
Do not report tab openings or pop-ups
