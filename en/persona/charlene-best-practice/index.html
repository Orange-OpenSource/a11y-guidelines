Good digital accessibility practices for Charlène

Good Reflexes Common mistakes
Form

Provide imprecise error messages
Animation
Allow control of animated content
Automatically launch animations
Captcha

Layout
Set up identical navigation on all pages
Propose long texts without any titles and subtitles
Police
Limit italics, underlining, and capitalized sentences
